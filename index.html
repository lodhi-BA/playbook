Delivery Framework

A repeatable four-phase model that merges analytical rigor with go-to-market execution. Each phase produces a concrete, measurable output — not just activity.

Phase 01
Discovery & Strategic Alignment
Define & Plan
BA
Translate business needs into data requirements, define KPIs, and identify stakeholders.
PM / GTM
Define the Ideal Customer Profile, validate the business case, and map the buyer journey. Identify the one or two core segments to target initially.
Goal Clear, measurable objectives connecting analytics to revenue.
Phase 02
Data Engineering & Insight Generation
Develop & Test
BA
Perform data exploration, preprocessing, and model building to address the defined objectives.
PM / GTM
Analyze data signals to refine targeting and messaging. Use data-driven insights to determine pricing structure and optimal sales model.
Goal A verified, actionable insight — a dashboard or predictive model ready for deployment.
Phase 03
Operationalization & Launch
Deploy & Execute
BA
Create dashboards and reports, validate against requirements, and present findings to stakeholders.
PM / GTM
Execute the GTM plan across two or three key channels. Implement sales enablement tools including playbooks and demo scripts.
Goal Successful deployment of the solution and launch of the product or segment strategy.
Phase 04
Measurement & Iteration
Operate & Optimize
BA
Perform ongoing data monitoring to identify root causes of issues and track performance against defined KPIs.
PM / GTM
Review CRM data, conversion rates, and ROI metrics on a weekly cadence. Use feedback loops from sales and customers to iterate on messaging and product improvements.
Goal Continuous improvement cycle with clear accountability at each iteration.
